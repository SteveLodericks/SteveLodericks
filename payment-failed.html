Common Payment Issues & Solutions

Card Declined

Contact your bank to ensure international transactions are enabled and your card isn't blocked.

iDEAL Not Working

Ensure you have sufficient funds and try logging into your bank's website first to verify access.

Apple/Google Pay Issues

Verify your default payment method in your device settings and ensure it's properly configured.

Browser Problems

Try clearing your browser cache, disabling ad blockers, or using a different browser.
