My Approach to Somatic Healing

Every individual's journey toward embodied wellness is unique. My approach honors your body's innate wisdom while providing evidence-based tools for lasting transformation.

Core Principles

My work is grounded in trauma-informed care, recognizing that true healing happens through the body. I integrate multiple somatic modalities to address the root causes of stress, trauma, and disconnection.

What to Expect

Sessions typically begin with gentle awareness practices, move through targeted somatic interventions, and conclude with integration and home practice planning. Each session is tailored to your current capacity and healing goals.
