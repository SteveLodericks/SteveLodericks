Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about somatic coaching and getting started

What is somatic coaching?

Somatic coaching works with the body's wisdom to address trauma, stress, and disconnection. It helps regulate the nervous system through movement, breath, and awareness practices.

Is this right for me?

Somatic work is beneficial for anyone experiencing stress, anxiety, trauma effects, or seeking deeper self-connection. A free consultation helps determine fit.

What happens in a session?

Sessions include gentle movement, breathing practices, and awareness exercises. Everything is voluntary and adapted to your comfort level and healing goals.

Online vs. in-person?

Both formats are effective. Online sessions offer convenience and accessibility, while in-person provides additional tactile and spatial elements.

How many sessions needed?

Varies by individual goals. Some benefit from single sessions, while deeper transformation often happens over 6-12 sessions. We'll discuss your specific needs.

Payment and insurance?

Session fees are paid directly. While not covered by standard insurance, some supplemental health plans may reimburse. Payment plans available for packages.
