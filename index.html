About Nicoline

Your guide to embodied healing and somatic awareness

Welcome to Your Journey Home

I'm Nicoline Thijssen, a certified somatic coach based in The Hague, dedicated to helping you reconnect with your body's innate wisdom. Through years of training in somatic experiencing, nervous system regulation, and embodied movement, I've witnessed the profound transformation that occurs when we learn to truly listen to our bodies.

My approach integrates cutting-edge neuroscience with ancient wisdom traditions, creating a safe container for you to explore, heal, and reclaim your authentic self. Whether you're dealing with trauma, chronic stress, anxiety, or simply feeling disconnected from your body, I'm here to support your journey back to wholeness.

Qualifications & Training

  • Certified Somatic Experiencing Practitioner (SEP)
  • Advanced Training in Nervous System Regulation
  • Certified in Embodied Movement Therapy
  • Trauma-Informed Body Work Certification
  • Master's in Psychology, Specialization in Body-Mind Integration
